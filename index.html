Welcome To My Website!

For over 20 years I had a career as a professional classical singer. It seemed to be a natural transition for me into making jewelry. Both are creative outlets and require precision to detail, and a love of self expression through storytelling. I am passionate about creating pieces that evoke a feeling of wonder that inspires people. I love collaborating with customers which sometimes can result in a truly magical process!
